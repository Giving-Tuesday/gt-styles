Key Findings

Finding 1

Digital Giving Dominates

  • 78% of donations made online
  • Mobile giving up 23%
  • Social media referrals doubled
  • Peer-to-peer fundraising grew 45%
78%

Finding 2

Gen Z Rising

Young donors (18-25) showed the highest growth rate in participation:

  • 42% increase in Gen Z donors
  • Average gift: $45
  • Most likely to donate via social media
  • Strong preference for cause-specific giving
Finding 3

Local Impact Focus

Donors increasingly want to see tangible local impact:

  • Community foundations +28%
  • Local food banks +45%
  • Neighborhood programs +32%
